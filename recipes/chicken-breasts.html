Description

Turn bone-in chicken breast into a golden, garlicky, and juicy meal with this 5-star recipe. Cook up deliciously seasoned and crispy chicken that turns a simple dinner into a sensational meal in a few easy steps. You'll be amazed at how quickly this dish comes together. Learn all about this impressive oven-baked chicken recipe, including how long to cook the chicken breast and how to store leftovers.

Enjoy an herb-infused chicken dish that doesn't shy away from big flavor. You'll find the full recipe below with step-by-step instructions:

Ingredients

  • 2 large bone-in chicken breast halves with skin
  • ¼ cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • ½ teaspoon garlic, minced
  • ½ teaspoon coarse sea salt
  • ½ teaspoon cracked black pepper
  • ¼ teaspoon dried rosemary
  • ¼ teaspoon dried basil

Steps

  1. Step 1: Gather all the ingredients
  2. Step 2: Rub chicken breasts with olive oil and garlic; sprinkle with salt, black pepper, rosemary, and basil. Arrange chicken in a large baking dish.
  3. Step 3: Refrigerate for atleast 45 minutes. Meanwhile, preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C)
  4. Step 4: Bake in the preheated oven until chicken meat is no longer pink at the bone and the juices run clear, 45 to 60 minutes. An instant-read thermometer inserted in the thickest part of the breast meat should read 165 degrees F (75 degrees C).
  5. Step 5: Serve hot and Enjoy!
